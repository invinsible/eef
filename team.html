president

Esports Europe is built to reflect the digital change and deliberative democratic formats of the 21st century. The EEF understands itself as a moderating partner of esports stakeholders rather than a governing body, thus aiming for bringing together national federations, stakeholders and esports ambassadors. In the end, the organization is looking for the best outcome for players in the esports ecosystem, especially for grassroots movements in esports.
